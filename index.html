Do you want to learn about the major subjects of BSEMC? or would you like to review some lessons? Here is QuRE, a learning site for BS Entertainment Multimedia Computing.

Start Learning

Quiz maker

The Quiz Maker is an intuitive feature that enables teachers and quiz creators to design engaging quizzes by specifying a title, setting the number of questions, and inputting questions with their corresponding answers. It streamlines the quiz creation process, saving time and ensuring a seamless experience.

Take quiz

Allows students to access and attempt quizzes created by teachers or quiz creators. It provides a user-friendly interface where students can select a quiz, answer questions, and receive immediate feedback on their performance. This feature is designed to enhance learning by enabling students to test their knowledge, identify areas for improvement, and track progress over time.

Reviewer

Designed to help students effectively prepare and reinforce their knowledge. It provides access to past quizzes, allowing students to review questions, correct answers, and explanations where available. This feature aims to support continuous learning by enabling students to revisit challenging topics, track their progress, and improve their understanding through self-assessment and repetition.
